Top Ten Dinner Spots in Ann Arbor

Whether you are looking for a fancy dinner or a quick bite to eat, these resturants are sure to leave you full and happy! These place are sure to leave you, your friends, your date, or your parents wanting more.

Biercamp

The best meats, cheese & sandwich hub with indoor-outdoor seating, house-brewed beers & a retail area! Check out Biercamp today!

Bigilora

Bigalora is one of the best contemporary Italian eatery providing Neapolitan, wood-fired pizzas, plus small plates & gelato.

Blue Tractor

Blue Tractor is a warm brewpub offers house craft beers paired with BBQ, smoked meats & biscuits amid live music.

Cafe Zola

Electic fare & global wines are served amid a rustic space with high ceilings & exposed brick at Cafe Zola.

Chelas

Chelas is known for the best authentic mexican street food in the area!

Isalita

Isalita is known for thier mexican standards served in a rustic space with painted-brick walls & a festive streetside patio.

Knights

Known for legacy of great food and service, exceptional value and a welcoming atmosphere. The Knight's tradition lives on today with our famously potent cocktails and signature dishes that continue to make our customers feel at home.

Mani Osteria and Bar

A local favorite for wood-oven pizza & Italian small plates plus bar with old-world wines & local beer in an open setup, Mani Osteria is a great spot to try!

The Pretzel Bell

The Pretzel Bell is a historic Ann Arbor resturant offering American comfort food & drinks amid Michigan memorabilia.

Slurping Turtle

Recently re-opened, Slurping Turtle, is an industrial-chic Japanese choice with long communal tables for tapas, noodles & sushi, plus sake.
