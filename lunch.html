Top Ten Lunch Spots in Ann Arbor

No matter what area of Ann Arbor you are in, these resturants are sure to satisfy! We have selected these resturants based on price, location, and overall taste of food!

Avalon

Avalon is a favorite for students, parents, locals, and everyone inbetween! They locally source ingredients to make thier amazing pastries and dishes!

Blimpy's Burgers

This nationally recognized resturant is knowns around the world for the amazing burgers and funny chefs! You won't regret visitng Blimpy's Burgers!

The Brown Jug

This student-favorite pub, The Brown Jug, was founded in 1936 serving Greek food & decorated in hometown college football memorabilia.

Detriot Street Filling Station

The Detriot Street Filling Station is a casual cafe with art-lined walls serving creative comfort fare, plenty of vegan dishes & cocktails!

Fresh Forage

This farm to table resturant uses locally-sourced ingredients to make beautiful, composed dishes that reflect the season. Stop by Fresh Forage for a good time!

Frita Batidos

Frita Batidos is known by locals, students, and everyone inbetween! They are know for serving cuban street food & tropical cocktails!

Jerusalem Garden

Jerusalem Garden is known for thier plentiful portions of Lebanese fare plus Turkish coffees

Pizza House

Another student favorite, Pizza House! This laid-back pizzera serves deep-dish pies & other Italian eats

Red Hawk Diner

The Red Hawk Diner located on State Street is known for making everything from dressings and desserts to stocks and sauces in house from scratch

Zingerman's

As the saying goes, a trip to Michigan is not complete without going to Zingerman's!
