Top Ten Breakfasts in Ann Arbor

Whether you are on State Street, Main Street, or East Washington, the 10 breakfast restaurants below are something you don't want to miss!

Stray Hen Cafe

Stray Hen offers a variety of classic American breakfasts, along with specialty coffees. This Ann Arbor favorite is both a colorful and comforting spot to bring your family and friends.

Angelos

On the corner of Catherine St and Glen Ave, Angelo's is the place to go for omelettes, coffee, and more!

Fleetwood Diner

Fleetwood Diner takes you back to the 60s, with a classic Diner feel and spectacular lunch foods.

Jagged Fork

Jagged Fork is the talk of town! Whether you're looking for a sweet stack of pancakes or a savory potato skillet, stop by Jagged Fork to fill you up!

aMa Bistro

aMa Bistro offers a huge menu of classic, home-style foods, and exciting Albanian and international dishes to suit every taste. Serving breakfast, lunch, and dinner.

Amer's

Amer's Deli offers a variety of foods and drinks ranging from classics, including chicken sandwhiches, to modern favorites, including smoothie bowls.

Anna's House

Dedicated to providing fresh, local, quality ingredients in our dishes, Anna's House has been family owned & operated in Michigan since 2002.

First Bite

Not only does First Bite offer some of the best breakfast foods in town, they also provide a great atmosphere and reasonable prices.

Afternoon Delight

This cafe in the heart of Ann Arbor is well know for its cozy atmosphere, buffet style breakfast, and their fan faorite eggs Benedict.

Juicy Kitchen

With their goal of providing healthy and creative breakfasts, the Juicy Kitchen of Ann Arbor is the new 'it' spot!
